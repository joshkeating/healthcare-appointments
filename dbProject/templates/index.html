About Us

Started in 2019, we wanted to build a platform that helps patients schedule quick appointments with their doctors and keep track of their prescriptions. By allowing patients to chat in real time with their providers, they can eliminate the need for time consuming visits.

Healthcare professionals are given an easy way to mange their patients medications and prescriptions. In giving providers the ability to prescribe medications immediately after an e-appointment, they cut down on time consuming administrative work
